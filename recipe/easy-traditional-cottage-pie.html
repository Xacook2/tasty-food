Cottage Pie

  • Cook: 1hr 20mins
  • Prep: 20mins
  • Total: 1hr 40mins
  • Serves: 6-8

Nutritional Information

  • kcal 645
  • Protein 35g
  • Carbs 40g
  • Fat 39g

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into chunks.
  2. Fill a large saucepan with water, add a pinch of salt, and bring it to a boil. Add the potatoes and cook until they are tender.
  3. While the potatoes are cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the chopped onions and garlic to the skillet and cook until they are soft and translucent.
  5. Stir in the carrots and mushrooms and cook for a few minutes until they begin to soften.
  6. Add the tomato purée, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, and oregano. Pour in the red wine if using. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 10 minutes.
  7. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  8. Drain and mash the cooked potatoes with butter and milk. Season with salt and pepper.
  9. Transfer the beef and vegetable mixture to a large baking dish. Spread the mashed potatoes over the top.
  10. Sprinkle the grated cheese over the mashed potatoes.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  12. Let the cottage pie cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
