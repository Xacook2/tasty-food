Cottage Pie

  • Cook: 1hr 20mins
  • Prep: 20mins
  • Total: 1hr 40mins
  • Serves: 6-8

Nutritional Information

  • kcal 645
  • Protein 35g
  • Carbs 40g
  • Fat 39g

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into chunks.
  2. Fill a large saucepan with water, add a pinch of salt, and bring it to a boil. Add the potatoes and cook until they are tender (about 20 minutes).
  3. While the potatoes are cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add 1kg ground beef and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the chopped onions and garlic to the skillet and cook until they are soft and translucent.
  5. Stir in 200g carrots and 300g mushrooms, and cook for a few minutes until they begin to soften.
  6. Add 2 tablespoons tomato purée, 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce, 1.5 teaspoons thyme, and 2 teaspoons dried oregano. Pour in 1 small glass of red wine if using. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 10 minutes.
  7. Preheat your oven to 190°C (375°F).
  8. Drain and mash the cooked potatoes with 50g butter and 175ml milk. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Transfer the beef and vegetable mixture to a large baking dish. Spread the mashed potatoes over the top.
  10. Sprinkle 200g grated cheese over the mashed potatoes.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  12. Let the cottage pie cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
